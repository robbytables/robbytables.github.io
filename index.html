Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to pay up-front?
I ask for 25% to hold your place in the cohort. The remainder is due before the start of Day 1 of the cohort.
How do you decide to admit someone to the cohort?
The cohort will heavily be based on my coaching style, which is fairly accesible to engineers of all backgrounds. The intro call will be important to make sure that the group setting and my style will work for you- my goal is to set you and the cohort up for success.
How much time commitment is required each week?
You will be required to participate in 2 hour of group sessions per week, and at least 1 hour of 1:1 time with Robby. Expect there to be homework, but the amount is on a per-case basis. Remember that this is a part of your winning strategy- the rest will be on you, but the more you bring to the cohort the more you will get out of it!
What if I can't attend a session?
Life happens, so if there is an emergency or an illness and you cannot make it, that's ok. Group sessions cannot be made up- though you may have access to summaries and/or recordings. Up to 1 hour of 1:1 coaching may be "rolled over" to the next week with Robby- but this should only be on an emergent basis.
What happens if I don't find a job during the program?
It's not guaranteed that you will find a job during this program. Over 50% of my coaching clients find employment during the first 30 days of us working together, but everyone's journey is unique. I can guarantee that you will gain indispensible skills and insights that will bootstrap your job search and be indispensible in all stages of your career. Cohort members will receive first dibs on joining further cohorts, and may be eligible for a discount as well.
What level of engineers do you work with?
I work with software engineers of all level, as long as they have some form of industry experience. Whether you hold a 4 year CS degree, bootcamp education, or are self taught, all are welcome. SWEs of all backgrounds, industries, and technical stacks will find value in this program.
