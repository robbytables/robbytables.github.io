My Story

I've spent years navigating the tech industry as both an engineer and a leader. I know what it's like to struggle with imposter syndrome, to wonder if you're being paid fairly, or to feel stuck in your career progression. That's exactly why I became a career coach.

My mission is simple: to level the playing field and make high-quality career coaching accessible to everyone, especially underrepresented groups in tech. I believe that with the right guidance, every engineer can land their dream job and build the career they deserve.

When I'm not coaching, you can find me contributing to open source projects, sharing career tips on social media, or mentoring the next generation of engineers. I'm passionate about building a more inclusive and equitable tech industry, one career at a time.
