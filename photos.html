Active Listening Photography

I'm Robby, aka @actlis_lens, and I love going to concerts. I especially love being up close in the action, but due to the inevitable march forward of time, I've traded in my mosh card for a photography pass. Living in NYC I have access to an amazing wealth of music and culture and I love capturing it through photography. I called my account Active Listening because I believe photography should convey the experience of being at the show, hearing what you see. If you're interested in having me shoot your show, reach out- I love trading edited photos for a photo pass, I don't charge for this as I only shoot shows I'd attend anyway. Also available for promo shoots, headshots, hanging out with your pets and taking cute photos of them, or whatever!
