Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start the Elevate program?
Ideally, start before your first day or within your first week. However, if you're already a few weeks in and feeling overwhelmed, it's not too late. We can adapt the program to meet you where you are.
How is this different from 1:1 coaching?
The Elevate program is specifically designed for onboarding success with a structured 90-day focus. It includes round-the-clock availability for emergent situations and a comprehensive framework for mastering your first quarter.
What does "round-the-clock availability" mean?
For emergent situations that require quick guidance—like an unexpected conflict, a high-stakes meeting, or a critical decision—you'll have access to reach out for support between our weekly sessions.
Can my company pay for this?
Yes! Many companies have L&D (Learning & Development) or Personal Development budgets for employees. I can provide documentation and work with you to help secure sponsorship from your employer.
What if I need to extend beyond 3 months?
At the end of the program, we'll review your progress and discuss next steps. If you'd like to continue working together, we can transition to standard 1:1 coaching or create a custom package.
Is this only for people at big tech companies?
No! The Elevate program is designed for engineers at all types of companies—whether you're joining a FAANG, a startup, a scale-up, or anywhere in between. I tailor the approach to your specific environment.
