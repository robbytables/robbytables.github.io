Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a session?
Simply fill out the form below and I'll respond within 24 hours to schedule your session.
What's the difference between 1:1 coaching and the cohort program?
1:1 coaching is completely personalized to your specific needs and timeline, while the cohort provides group learning and peer support. 1:1 is perfect for targeted challenges or if you prefer individual attention.
How long are the coaching sessions?
Standard sessions are 60 minutes, which provides enough time to dive deep into topics while maintaining focus. The initial consultation is 30 minutes to understand your needs.
Can I switch between single sessions and monthly packages?
Absolutely! Many clients start with a single session to see if we're a good fit, then move to monthly packages for ongoing support. We can adjust based on your needs and budget.
What if I need to reschedule a session?
Life happens! I ask that you give me 24 hours notice and we can reschedule, no penalty. I want to make sure you get the most value from each session.
Do you offer payment plans?
For monthly packages and extended coaching relationships, I'm happy to discuss flexible payment options. My goal is to make coaching accessible while ensuring you're committed to the process.
